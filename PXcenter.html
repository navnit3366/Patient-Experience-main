Patient Experience (PX) Improvement Portal


HealthOrg is committed to providing outstanding experiences to patients throughout the service delivery process. This portal shows the results of surveys completed by patients within 1 week of an office visit. Surveys are administered on an ongoing basis, and improvements toward our goals are tracked over time.


When we started the PX initiative four quarters ago, the average patient was neutral about recommending their medical center to friends or on social media. Currently, the average respondent is "likely" to recommend their center.
